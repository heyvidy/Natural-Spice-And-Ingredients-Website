About Natural Spice And Ingredients

THE BEST SPICE MANUFACTURERS IN INDIA

Manufacture and process all your spice needs, with best practices & highest quality standards in the industry.

Our aim at Natural Spices and Ingredients is to provide top grade masala and spices to institutions. Procuring raw materials from reliable sources who use good cultivation practices has been our driving force. What began as an objective by Mr.Uday, a chemical engineer, to combat the lack of quality spices available at institutions, has now grown into a conscious company over the course of two decades.

At Natural Spices & Ingredients, hygiene and quality are of utmost importance and we strive to implement them at every stage in every process. Over the years, our commitment to stringent quality checks and affordable pricing, has allowed us to have long standing relationships with our esteemed vendors and customers.

We have the facility to perform bulk packing & private labelling. The success of Natural Spices and Ingredients in bulk supplies has paved the way for our company to expand into consumer packs under the brand name PK which has been listed on Amazon and is also available in selected stores across India.
