Manufacturing Services

Procurement

Great food starts with great ingredients. Our customers enjoy only the most authentic of eastern flavours, as we procure high quality produce from the best agriculturists across India.

De-stoning

Post procurement, raw materials are subjected to destoning, where impurities like stones, pebbles, glass shards and other high density forgeign objects are eliminated for the next stage of processing.

Roasting

Whole spices, grains and pulses are roasted based on requirement to enhance the flavor and aroma of the final product.

Blending

We blend different spices and herbs to create exquisite spice blends for ready-made cooking. Blending the right kind of spices also helps enrich the flavor of the blend.

Grinding

Spices play an important role in enhancing the flavor and taste of the processed foods. We grind spices to obtain desired consistency with good product quality in terms of flavoring and color.

Fumigation

To maintain high quality, the produce is treated to a process of pest eradication by application of tested and approved fumigants.

Sieving

All grounded spices, pluses and grains are sieved to maintain uniformity of the grounded material by passing through different mesh sizes as per customer requirement to obtain the desired product.

Packing

All the above processes go in vain if the spices are not packed properly. We make sure that our products receive top notch packaging, considering their sacrosanct nature.
