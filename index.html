Manufacturing Services

Procurement

The better the spices you have, the better the product you can manufacture. We procure best quality spices from different places all over India which makes your products better and tastier.

Fumigation

The spices we use are fumigaed for pest control by using tested fumigants to suffocate the pests within. Fumigation makes the plants and spices pest free which might cause infections to humans.

De-stoning

Spices often contains stones, pebbles, glass, and other high density impurities. We de-sotne the grains, seeds and spices to ensure we manufacture the best quality spices.

Sieving

We sieve all the spices that we procure and sieve to separate wanted elements from unwanted materials and also for characterizing the particle size distribution for variety of spices.

Roasting

Roasting your spices is a simple way to bring an almost magical flavor to your foods. We roast the spices to create a greater depth of flavour and robustness which makes your food yummy.

Grinding

Spices play an important role in enhancing the flavor and taste of the processed foods. We grind spices to obtain smaller particle size with good product quality in terms of flavoring and color.

Blending

We blend different spices and herbs to create spice mixes beforehand so that you can use it directly to your food. Blending the right spices increases the rich aroma, flavor and taste of the food.

Packing

All the above process goes in vain if the spices aren’t packed properly. We make sure that our packagign is top notch so that you can use the best quality of spices to make the food.
